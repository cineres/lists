About announce

Low volume announcement list that includes things like new SIGs (Special Interest Groups), special events and other important information about Buffalo Lab.

Also check out our blog at http://www.buffalolab.org/blog/ To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the announce Archives.

Using announce

To post a message to all the list members, send email to announce@lists.buffalolab.org.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.

Subscribing to announce

Subscribe to announce by filling out the form on the side. You will be sent email requesting confirmation, to prevent others from gratuitously subscribing you. This is a hidden list, which means that the list of members is available only to the list administrator.
